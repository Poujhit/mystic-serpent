General Power-Up Mechanics

Power-ups have a 30% chance to spawn instead of normal food (powerUpChance = 0.3).

When a power-up spawns:

  1. First checks if shrink power-up should spawn based on snake length
  2. If not shrink, randomly selects from the available power-up types
  3. Power-up timers are managed by the updatePowerUps() method
  4. Multiple power-ups can be active simultaneously
